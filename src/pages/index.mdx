# Latvia vACC Knowledgebase

This collection of resources is a guide and source of theoretical knowledge (basically a Wiki) for Air Traffic Control students of Latvia vACC on the VATSIM Network as well as anyone else curious or interested in expanding their knowledge about the work that happens behind the scopes in EVRR FIR. If you are here for the first time, just click one of the cards below to find out more about topics that interest you!

Latvia vACC Knowledgebase is maintained jointly by the Training Department and the Technical Department of the vACC.

Should you have any technical issues, such as visual bugs (e.g. content rendering issues), or should the contents of the knowledgebase be inaccurate or out of date, refer to the links on the right side of all content pages within the knowledgebase - through those you will be able to report the issue on GitHub. In case you do not have a GitHub account and wish not to make one, forward your concerns to the Latvia vACC Technical Department at tech@lv-vacc.org.
