This collection of resources is a guide and source of theoretical knowledge (basically a Wiki) for Air Traffic Control students of Latvia vACC on the VATSIM Network as well as anyone else curious or interested in expanding their knowledge about the work that happens behind the scopes of the EVRR FIR.
