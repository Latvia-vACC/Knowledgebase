# vATIS Guide

In accordance with the [Air Traffic Control Frequency and Information Management Policy](https://vatsim.net/docs/policy/air-traffic-control-frequency-and-information-management-policy) any controller is allowed to facilitate up to 4 ATIS connections on VATSIM. vATIS is the designated ATIS hosting client in EVRR FIR. It comes in pair with a dedicated config designed for use within EVRR FIR. Both are available for download by clicking the buttons below.

## Usage Guide

In short, vATIS is not overly complex. To use the client appropriately, only 4 steps have to be followed:

1. Import the EVRR FIR Config.
2. Select the runway in use and preferred type of approach.
3. Apply appropriate `NOTAMs` and `Airport Conditions` by clicking the relevant labels.
4. Press `Connect`!

Further reading and additional information is available in the [vATIS official Documentation](https://vatis.app/docs/).
